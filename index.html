About Us

The Mission of Christ International Church is a Christian ministry based in Toronto, Canada. Our calling is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, disciple believers, and serve communities through spiritual and practical support.

Our Mission

We focus on evangelism, prayer revival, discipleship training, and supporting newcomers and immigrant families as they build a new life in Canada.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed through the power of the Gospel and raise disciples who will carry the message of Christ to the nations.
